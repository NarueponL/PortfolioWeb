Project : Chicken Run

About this Game

Run as fast as you can into different dimensions for eternity.

Chicken Run is a 2D Action Side Scrolling game where players become a roast chicken who must run away from a Reaper who comes to take your life.

How to Play

Chicken

You are a Chicken
All you have to do is "RUN"

Reaper

This is Reaper the Chicken Killer
Don't get caught or you will lose

Coin

Collect all the coins as required
That's your mission

Portal

The portal will open only when you
have collected the required amount of coins
